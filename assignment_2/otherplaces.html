The Rideau Canal

Ottawa is the capital of Canada and has many beautiful places to visit. The Rideau Canal is one of the most beautiful places in the city to see and it's known worldwide for being the world's largest skating rink during winter.

Gatineau

Gatineau is a small town in Western Quebec and it is part of Canada's National Capital Region being located immediately accross the Ottawa river.
